For professional investors only.

Our long heritage in responsible investment includes the launch of Europe’s first ethical investment fund in 1984. Today we have one of the largest Governance and Sustainable Investment (GSI) teams in Europe and offer a suite of funds investing across a range of asset types. We continue to innovate and were also the first asset manager to use an independent advisory body to oversee our ethical screening process. This body is presided over by the Archbishop of Canterbury, The Most Reverend Justin Welby.

Our GSI team

With extensive experience across a range of ESG issues and sectors, the responsibilities of our GSI team include:

  • researching emerging ESG issues to help inform policy development
  • reviewing companies against the ethical criteria
  • voting at company meetings and engaging with companies, regulators and other stakeholders to improve corporate governance and corporate sustainability

Responsible Investment Advisory Council

A six-strong independent Responsible Investment Advisory Council oversees the screening criteria within which our funds are managed.

Who are BMO Global Asset Management?

We are the asset management arm of BMO (Bank of Montreal), Canada’s second largest bank. Today we manage over £190 billion in assets (at 31.10.17), and have more than 1,200 employees worldwide with our clients gaining access to the experience and local knowledge of teams in our investment centres in London, Chicago, Toronto and Hong Kong.

Risk factors

The value of investments and any income derived from them can go down as well as up as a result of market or currency movements and investors may not get back the original amount invested. Screening out sector or companies may result in less diversification and hence more volatility in investment values.
